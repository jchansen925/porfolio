We help you to build your projects on the strongest foundation

Proper site development begins with an accurate understanding of a site's geologic history and structure. Start your project from the ground up with our expert engineering geologist.

Engineering Geology Services

Easton Geology has over two decades of experience with engineering geologic projects for all land use types in geologically challenging Northern and Central California terrain.

Geologic Hazards Assessment (GHA)

One of the first steps in planning development is identifying potential geologic hazards. A GHA by Easton Geology does just that.

Landslide & Slope Stability Investigations

Development in mountainous areas may encounter existing landslides, or new ones can form.

Faulting & Earthquake Hazards Investigations

Risks from seismic shaking can be significant, and developments within active fault zones are at risk from ground rupture.

Erosion & Coastal Engineering Studies

The shoreline retreats at different rates in different places, and sometimes, it seemingly erodes all at once.

Timber Harvest Assessments

Sustainable lumber promotes a healthy forest. Proper attention to areas considered for timber harvesting helps prevent erosion and more.

Leachfield Investigation & Septic System Design

The soil plays a crucial role in absorbing and treating wastewater and is a significant factor in properly sizing your septic drain field.
